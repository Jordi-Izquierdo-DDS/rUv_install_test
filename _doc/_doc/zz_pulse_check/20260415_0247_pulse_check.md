## 5. Actionable findings (sorted by impact)

### HIGH — reward signal flatness

**Symptom:** all patterns crystallize with `avg_quality=0.80` regardless of trajectory content. Learning has no gradient.

**Hypothesis:** `hook-handler.cjs` passes a uniform reward to `end_trajectory`. Either:
- PostToolUse rewards (±0.1) aren't aggregating into the final trajectory-close reward.
- `c.reward ?? 0` default in `daemon.end_trajectory` is being overridden to a constant from hook code.

**Action:** trace reward flow PostToolUse → daemon.add_step(reward) → end_trajectory(reward). If rewards are +0.1 per successful step and the trajectory close uses avg(step_rewards), expected quality would be ≈0.1, not 0.80. The 0.80 observation suggests `end_trajectory` is being called with a hard-coded 0.8 somewhere.

### MEDIUM — `intelligence.cjs` formatter output

**Symptom:** `[SONA] pattern [rank #N] (0.00)` — score always zero, no pattern identity visible.

**Action:** read `intelligence.cjs` format string, check which `JsLearnedPattern` field it's reading. Swap to `avg_quality + pattern_type + cluster_size`. ≤10 LOC change.

### MEDIUM — `pattern_type="General"` universal

**Symptom:** categorization not propagating from `VerdictAnalyzer` into the persisted pattern record.

**Action:** verify whether (a) VerdictAnalyzer's output category is being stored on the pattern vs the trajectory, or (b) the napi_simple.rs wrapper isn't copying it through. May correlate with reward-flatness above.

### LOW — `access_count=0` on both patterns and C4 entries

**Symptom:** retrieval counters don't increment.

**Action:** next rebuild of sona, check whether `reasoning_bank.find_similar` bumps `access_count` in-memory. If yes, the issue is saveState not persisting it. If no, that's upstream behavior (informational).

### LOW — cluster_size=1 universal

**Self-corrects at scale.** Nothing to do until trajectory corpus exceeds ~50 (SonaConfig `pattern_clusters` default).
